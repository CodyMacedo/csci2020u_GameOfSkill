								Instructions For PokeMatch 

This is a game that tests the users memory power. Cards will have to be matched based on appearence and it will be timed.If the user correctly matches
a pair of cards both cards will show, on the other hand if the user doesnt match the pair of cards only one card will show. The scores will be sent to a leaderboard
and the person with the fastest time will be on top of the leaderboard.There are three difficulties easy, medium, and hard. Easy is matching 8 pairs, medium is matching 20 pairs, and
hard is matching 40 pairs.
