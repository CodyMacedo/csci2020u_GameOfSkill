											About PokeMatch

The intention behind PokeMatch was to create a game that tests and strengthens memory. The proud developers of PokeMatch are Cody Macedo, Alex Ly, and Gayanath Sasitharanathan.
PokeMatch is a game for all age groups, it was inspired by Alex Ly who decided on the concept of the game, Gayanath Sasitharanathan created the User interface while Cody Macedo used 
sockets and servers to create a functional Leaderboard.
